About Braeden

Braeden's Story

Braeden's fascination with all that is comically oblong began in his early days. Pickles, decorative gourds, salami, and, yes zucchinis too, were all passions of a young Braeden. By High School he knew where his life would lead, and worked towards oblong proficiency. At the University of British Columbia he studied egg-shaped musical instruments. Using his skilled, loving, and nurturing soul to craft zucchini-perfection on his farm is his dream. He can often be heard saying around the farm "A life growing ovals is a life growing love." Braeden is available for corporate, school, and group motivational speaking.
