Location

Braeden's 10 acre farm is located in fertile Delta, British Columbia. Please visit us if you'd like to pick out a baby zucchini to sponsor from the vine. You'll be able to watch your zucchini grow via our app and see us care for your veggie-baby from the comfort of your own home.
